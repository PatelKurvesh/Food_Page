Vegetarian

The person who eats green foods is called vegetarian. It includes all plant based diets like fruits,vegetables,etc..It does not include meat,animal derived foods and by products.

Non-Vegetarian

A non-vegetarian diet includes the consumption of animal products,including meat,fish and other type of seafood.

Eggetarian

egg + vegetarian

Therefore, a person who eats Eggs but not animals calls himself an Eggetarian or a vegetarian who eats eggs is called an Eggetarian. The word “Eggetarian” is mostly used in India.
